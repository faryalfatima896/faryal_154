Intro:
"My name is Faryal Fatima and my journey to the Computer Engineering department at UET Faisalabad wasn't just an academic pursuit; it was a battle for survival. It started with the sweat and stress of the ECAT, the dream of securing a seat. When I finally got that admission, I thought the hard part was over. I was wrong. Here I was, a fresher in a new city, alone in a hostel, grappling with a trauma that felt impossible to process while being thrown into the deep end of practical labs and co-education dynamics. This portfolio isn't just about learning to code; it's about learning to stand up when life tries to bury you. It’s the story of how I turned grief into grit, one lecture and lab project at a time."
 (Semester 1 Journey)
1. Title: "The ECAT Gauntlet: Securing My Seat at UET FSD"
•	The Dream and the Grind: Staying up late to solve past papers, the pressure of competing with thousands of students for a single seat. It wasn't just an exam; it was a ticket to a future I was building for myself and my family.
•	The Result Day Panic: The anxiety of checking the merit list, the sheer relief and joy when "Computer Engineering, UET FSD" popped up on the screen. A moment of pure victory, feeling like all the hard work paid off.
•	The Bittersweet Goodbye: The excitement of packing my bags mixed with the guilt of leaving home. I didn't know it then, but it would be the last time I left home with my world still intact.
